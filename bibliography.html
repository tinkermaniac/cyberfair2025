Cultural Studies

The Meaning of "Umeed" in Urdu

https://www.quora.com/What-does-the-Urdu-word-umeed-mean

An exploration of the Urdu word "Umeed," which means hope, expectation, or aspiration. This source provides cultural context for our project's name and mission.

A Beacon of Hope

https://faithgateway.com/blogs/christian-books/a-beacon-of-hope

This article explores the concept of hope as a guiding light in difficult times, which aligns with our project's mission to bring hope to the elderly.

Differences Between Indian Culture and Western Culture

https://avanigo.com/difference-between-indian-culture-and-western-culture/

A comparative analysis of Indian and Western cultural values, particularly regarding family structures and elder care, which informed our understanding of cultural contexts.

Culture of India

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Culture_of_India

A comprehensive overview of Indian culture, traditions, and values that provided background knowledge for our project's cultural context.

Mythological & Historical References

Shravana Kumara

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shravana_Kumara

Information about Shravana Kumara, a figure from Hindu mythology known for his devotion to his elderly parents, which serves as an inspiration for our project.

The Story of Shravan Kumar

https://www.chivukulas.com/2019/03/the-story-of-shravan-kumar.html

A detailed narrative of Shravan Kumar's story, highlighting the cultural importance of elder care in Indian tradition.

Valmiki Ramayana - Ayodhya Kanda

https://www.valmikiramayan.net/utf8/ayodhya/sarga63/ayodhya_63_frame.htm

Original text from the Ramayana that references the story of Shravan Kumar, providing historical context for our project's values.
